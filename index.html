About CBM Sales & Biz Solutions

CBM Sales & Biz Solution (CBMSBS) is a result driven-business support brand commited to helping entrepreneurs and business owners increase sales. We specialize in connecting sellers with active, ready-to-buy customers, reviving stagnant businesses and implementing straegies that deliver measurable growth. We don't just promote - we bring sales.

Our team

Our team of experts brings years of experience and a deep understanding of the intricacies of financial markets and strategic consulting. We pride ourselves on providing personalized solutions that meet the unique needs of each client, helping them navigate the complexities of business growth.

Our Core Principles

Mission Statement:

To drive sales by connecting entrepreneurs with active, ready-to-buy customers, To revive stagnant brands and increase brand visibility and to support business growth through strategic solutions that fuel sales locally and internationally.

Vision Statement:

To become Africa's top sales-driven brand that empowers entrepreneurs by transforming struggling businesses into profitable ones through smart strategies and real buyer connections - locally and internationally.

Core Goals/Objectives:

  • Connect businesses with active, ready-to-buy customers locally and internationally.
  • Revive stagnant brands with practical sales-first strategies
  • Boost visibility and attract consistent buyers for SMEs

  • Support Entrepreneurs with tools, leads and buyer matchmaking
  • Build a thriving community through partnerships, training and promotions
